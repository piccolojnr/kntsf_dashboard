# Table of contents

*(Per KUC guideline:)*

- The title **TABLE OF CONTENTS** (not just "Contents") should be typed in upper case and bold.
- All chapter headings should be in upper case letters.
- Sub-headings should appear in title case (first letters capitalised) and should be numbered.
- The corresponding page numbers of all headings and sub-headings should be indicated.
- The table of contents should be double-spaced.
